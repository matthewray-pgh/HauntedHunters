# EP10 - "OBSIDIAN - File 00"

## Pillar
Directorate Intelligence + Arc Payoff

## Lore
First Phantom confirmed, Arc 1 closes, Arc 2 seeded

## Summary

The Archivist releases a single document. One page. Obsidian classification. Most of it is redacted.
But one line is not.
Arc 1 ends. The community will not stop talking about it.

## Content Structure

- Cold open: black screen. Archivist annotation only:
  *"I should not have this."*
- Single document appears - OBSIDIAN stamp, FILE 00
- Slow pan across redacted fields - entity name, classification, origin, behavior, threat assessment - all black
- Related incidents field: Black Vein. Ashfall. Station 13. And one more - no date, no name, no location.
- One field is not redacted: PREDATES KNOWN VEIL ACTIVITY
- Final line - unredacted:
  *"It was here before the Veil."*
- Archivist final annotation: *"This is not the beginning of the file. This is the end of it."*
- Black screen. Silence. Roll credits with no music.

## Lore Introduced

- The First Phantom confirmed as real
- Predates the Veil itself
- Connected to all three historical incidents
- A fourth incident - unnamed - exists

## Arc 1 Payoff

The audience now knows:

- Something exists that predates everything
- The Directorate knows about it
- It has been in every incident
- The channel has been showing it to them all along

## Seeds Arc 2

- What is the fourth incident?
- What does "before the Veil" mean?
- Who is personnel number 7741?
- What was Unit 00?

## Ends On

Silence. Then - beneath the credits - the 1-second voice from EP03 plays again. Clearly. For the first time without static.
It says one word.
*"Found."*

## Thumbnail Concept

Black. OBSIDIAN stamp. One visible line of text. Nothing else.
