# EP09 - "The Ashfall Researcher - Personal Notes"

## Pillar
Recovered Log + Directorate Intelligence

## Lore
Ashfall deeper dive, First Phantom approach, Veil sentience theory

## Summary

A single researcher's personal notebook recovered from Ashfall Site C. Not an official document. Written observations that contradict the official project record - and suggest something responded to the experiment before it was activated.

## Content Structure

- Notebook pages presented - handwritten aesthetic, deteriorating
- Early entries: excitement, scientific optimism
- Middle entries: anomalies, equipment behaving incorrectly
- Late entries: the researcher describes something watching the team
- Final entry: *"It responded before we activated the interface. It was already listening."*
- Archivist annotation: *"This notebook was found outside the facility. The facility has no exits that remain accessible."*

## Lore Introduced

- First Phantom behavior - present before the breach, not caused by it
- Veil sentience theory elevated from theory to probable
- Ashfall recontextualized - they didn't open a door, something opened it from the other side

## First Phantom Fragment

The researcher's notes reference something that responded before the interface was activated - the clearest First Phantom reference yet. Still unnamed.

## Ends On

The final notebook page - blank except for one line drawn in what appears to be ash:
*"It knew we were coming."*

## Thumbnail Concept

Deteriorating notebook page. One line visible. Everything else burned away.
