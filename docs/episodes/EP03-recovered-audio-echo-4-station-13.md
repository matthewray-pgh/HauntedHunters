# EP03 - "Recovered Audio - Echo-4 - Station 13"

## Pillar
Recovered Log

## Lore
Station 13 Incident, Hunter mortality, The Conductor active

## Summary

The recovered audio from Hunter Team Echo-4's final deployment.
Presented as raw footage with minimal on-screen text.
The team does not make it out.

## Content Structure

- Static open - tape reel sound
- Audio log begins mid-conversation - team already underground
- Progressive environmental deterioration (lights, comms)
- The Conductor manifests through audio distortion
- Recording cuts - silence - Archivist annotation appears:
  *"This recording was scheduled for destruction. It was not destroyed."*

## Lore Introduced

- Station 13 Incident detailed
- Hunter team dynamics
- The Conductor's behavior in the field

## First Phantom Fragment

Beneath one static burst - 1 second of a voice not on the transcript. Different cadence. Different tone.

## Ends On

The sound of a train arriving. Doors opening. Silence.

## Thumbnail Concept

VHS static. Underground platform. Timestamp frozen.
