# EP07 - "The Red Platform - Station 13 Revisited"

## Pillar
Recovered Log + Entity File

## Lore
Station 13 deeper dive, Red Platform, The Conductor expanded

## Summary

Recovered maps from the Station 13 incident reference The Red Platform - a structure with no architectural record. This video investigates what it might be and what was found there.

## Content Structure

- Station 13 infrastructure maps - Red Platform marked
- Cross-reference with city transit authority records - no match
- Recovered photograph of the platform
- Archivist annotation: *"Three recovery teams were sent to document the Red Platform. Their findings are not in this file."*
- Expanded Conductor behavioral notes
- Distorted audio - platform ambient sound

## Lore Introduced

- Red Platform established as a genuine anomaly
- Conductor's connection to the platform deepened
- Recovery team disappearances - pattern emerging

## First Phantom Fragment

The platform photograph contains a reflection in the track surface - a figure standing behind the photographer. No one else was on the team.

## Ends On

A single recovered note from a recovery team member:
*"The platform goes further than the tunnel. I don't know how."*
