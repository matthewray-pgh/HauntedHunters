# EP05 - "The Hunters Directorate - Internal Structure"

## Pillar
Directorate Intelligence

## Lore
Directorate factions, internal conflict, Hunter expendability

## Summary

A leaked internal Directorate briefing document outlining organizational structure, division responsibilities, and the three ideological factions. The first hint that the Directorate is not unified.

## Content Structure

- Org chart document - Field Ops, Containment, Research, Recovery, Oversight
- Division responsibility breakdown
- Faction memo: The Wardens vs The Architects vs The Threshold Initiative
- Hunter mortality statistics - heavily redacted
- Archivist annotation: *"They don't agree on what the Veil is. They agree on keeping you from knowing it exists."*

## Lore Introduced

- Directorate internal factions in detail
- Hunter mortality framing - expendable but valuable
- First suggestion that the Directorate may be hiding things from itself

## First Phantom Fragment

A Directorate memo references a "prior occurrence" with no date, no file number, no classification stamp.

## Ends On

Final line of the faction memo - partially redacted:
*"The Threshold Initiative believes adaptation is [REDACTED]. The Wardens believe this is [REDACTED]. Both are [REDACTED]."*

## Thumbnail Concept

Organizational chart. Three faction names visible. Everything connecting them - blacked out.
