# Industrial Paranormal Horror — Creative Direction

## Status
Canon Creative Foundation

---

# Core Identity

Obsidian Frequency is defined by a distinct blend of industrial decay and paranormal intrusion.

The universe is not traditional horror, fantasy, or supernatural mythology.

Instead, it exists at the intersection of:
> collapsing industrial civilization and reality distortion events originating from The Veil.

This creates a grounded but unnatural horror tone.

---

# Core Aesthetic Pillars

## Rust Belt Decay
Abandoned industrial regions, aging infrastructure, and collapsing economic zones define the physical world.

- rusted factories
- empty steel mills
- decaying suburbs
- forgotten industrial towns

---

## Appalachian Horror Influence
Remote, isolated, and subterranean environments heavily shape the tone.

- mining disasters
- underground tunnels
- mountain infrastructure
- isolated communities
- sealed-off excavation sites

---

## Laboratories & Forbidden Science
Scientific environments are central to the Hunters Directorate and Veil research.

- underground research labs
- containment chambers
- experimental breach sites
- failed containment systems
- classified black projects

---

## Transit Systems
Transportation infrastructure becomes a recurring gateway for anomalies.

- subway systems
- rail tunnels
- maintenance corridors
- abandoned stations
- signal-controlled environments

---

## Mines & Subterranean Spaces
Deep earth environments are high-risk Veil interaction zones.

- collapsed tunnels
- excavation sites
- non-Euclidean underground structures
- sealed shafts
- unknown deep formations

---

## Flooded & Corrupted Environments
Water and decay amplify instability.

- flooded tunnels
- submerged laboratories
- leaking containment zones
- contaminated industrial runoff areas

---

## Abandoned Infrastructure
Human absence creates conditions for Veil emergence.

- empty hospitals
- derelict schools
- shut-down power stations
- abandoned government facilities

---

## Occult Science (Non-Fantasy Framing)
The universe does NOT use magic systems.

Instead, it uses:
> scientific interpretation of anomalous reality behavior.

- containment rituals interpreted as procedures
- anomaly classification systems
- experimental interaction with the Veil
- unknown physical laws

---

## Analog & Obsolete Technology
Technology reinforces tone and limitation.

- CRT monitors
- analog radios
- VHS surveillance
- printed reports
- mechanical systems
- early digital interfaces

---

# Narrative Function

These aesthetics are not just visual themes.

They directly support:
- entity design
- environmental storytelling
- gameplay mechanics
- mission structure
- and YouTube lore presentation

---

# Design Rule

Any new entity, location, or incident should reinforce at least one of the following:

- industrial decay
- isolation
- subterranean tension
- institutional secrecy
- environmental corruption
- analog technological limitations
- Veil interaction instability

If it does not reinforce these themes, it does not belong in canon.
