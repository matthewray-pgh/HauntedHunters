# What Arc 1 Establishes

By the end of Video 10, the audience knows:
- The Veil is real and dangerous
- The Directorate is compromised by internal faction war
- Three major historical incidents are connected
- The Hollow King is not the apex entity
- Something existed before the Directorate, before 1968, before the first breach
- The Archivist is afraid

## What Arc 1 Deliberately Withholds
- The First Phantom's nature, appearance, or intent
- What happened on the Red Platform
- Who approved the Ashfall experiments
- What is before 1968
- Whether the Archivist is still alive

These are Arc 2.
