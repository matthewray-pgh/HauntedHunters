# Video 09 - The Timeline Has a Gap

## Pillar
Directorate Intelligence / ARG Setup

## Lore Focus
Master timeline, pre-1968 history, what the Directorate is not saying

## Purpose
The audience has now seen the world, the entities, the incidents, and the factions.
This video pulls back further - to the timeline itself.
Something happened before 1968. Before the Black Vein Collapse.
Before the Directorate existed.
The Veil was not discovered in 1968. It was re-discovered.

## Format
- Directorate master timeline document
- Deliberate gaps and inconsistencies highlighted through pacing
- Archivist annotations more frequent than usual - they are agitated
- Ends on the blank pre-1968 entry

## Content Outline
- The official timeline - 1968 onward
- Inconsistencies in the record - language that implies prior knowledge
- Black Vein survivor testimony re-examined - "already there" language
- Ashfall researchers referencing "earlier contact events"
- The blank entry - a date before 1968, no content, not redacted, simply empty
- Closing Archivist annotation: *"The Directorate did not create a response to the Veil. They inherited one. I am trying to find out from whom."*

## First Phantom Seed
The blank timeline entry. A date. No content. The date is the only unredacted element on the page.

## Intended Viewer Reaction
*"The Veil has been known about for longer than they admit. What happened before 1968."*
