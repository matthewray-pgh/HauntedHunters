# Video 03 - The Black Vein Collapse

## Pillar
Incident Report

## Lore Focus
Black Vein Collapse 1968, Directorate origin, early Veil contact

## Purpose
First major historical incident breakdown.
Establish 1968 as the foundational trauma of the Directorate.
Seed the Hollow King and Carapax Prawn without naming them directly.
This is the video that makes the audience realize the world has deep roots.

## Format
- Classified incident report reading
- Archival photography aesthetic (grainy, damaged)
- Mining ambient sound design
- Survivor testimony fragments (text on screen, not voiced)

## Content Outline
- The excavation - what crews found
- The disappearances - clinical accounting of missing personnel
- Federal recovery teams - most did not return
- Reported phenomena - tunnel geometry, spatial looping, missing time
- Directorate formation - Black Vein as the catalyst
- Current status - officially sealed, unofficially active
- Closing Archivist annotation: *"Several records from this site remain unaccounted for. This is one of them."*

## First Phantom Seed
Survivor transcript quotes: *"the one that was already there."* No follow-up. No explanation.

## Intended Viewer Reaction
*"The Directorate was created because of this. And it's still active."*
