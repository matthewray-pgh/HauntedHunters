# Video 06 - The Hollow King

## Pillar
Entity File

## Lore Focus
Hollow King, apex entities, Veil escalation

## Purpose
First Legendary entity reveal.
Establish that some things cannot be contained - only documented.
Shift the audience's understanding of what the Directorate is actually facing.
This is the video where the world stops feeling manageable.

## Format
- Fragmented entity file - clearly incomplete
- Survivor account text overlays
- Deep forest ambient sound
- Subsonic drone underneath entire video

## Content Outline
- Classification - why Obsidian level exists
- Known behaviors - waiting, presence, passive escalation
- Environmental effects - plant death, time perception, equipment failure
- Connection to Black Vein lower levels
- Ashfall reference - "the one the breach opens for"
- Why no capture has ever been attempted
- Closing Archivist annotation: *"There is no containment protocol for this entity. There has never been one written."*

## First Phantom Seed
Entity file footnote references a redacted predecessor entity. Single line. Redaction bar slightly too short - one letter visible at the end.

## Intended Viewer Reaction
*"The Hollow King isn't the apex. Something came before it."*
