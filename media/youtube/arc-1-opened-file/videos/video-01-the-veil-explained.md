# Video 01 - The Veil Explained

## Pillar
Incident Report / World Introduction

## Lore Focus
The Veil, Veil Breaches, The Hunters Directorate

## Purpose
Establish the world without feeling like a world introduction.
Frame it as a leaked Directorate orientation document - the kind given to new operatives.
The audience is not being welcomed. They are reading something they were not supposed to find.

## Format
- Directorate internal document reading
- Ambient CRT visual treatment
- Sparse Archivist annotation at open and close

## Content Outline
- What the Veil is - Directorate definition, clinical language
- What Veil Breaches are - severity levels I through V
- What the Hunters Directorate is - brief, cold, institutional
- What Hunters are - expendable but valuable
- Closing Archivist annotation: *"This document is given to every new operative. Most of them are dead within two years."*

## First Phantom Seed
Personnel file number 0000 visible in document corner. No name. No clearance level.

## Intended Viewer Reaction
*"What is this channel. I need to watch the next one."*
