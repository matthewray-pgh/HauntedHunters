# Video 07 - The Directorate Factions

## Pillar
Directorate Intelligence

## Lore Focus
The Wardens, The Architects, The Threshold Initiative

## Purpose
Pull back the curtain on the Directorate's internal war.
The audience now understands the Veil is dangerous.
Now they learn that not everyone in the organization tasked with stopping it actually wants to stop it.
This recontextualizes Ashfall - and raises questions about who approved it.

## Format
- Leaked internal intelligence assessment
- Three faction dossiers - clinical, bureaucratic
- Redacted signatory on all documents (same length every time)
- Tension built through implication not action

## Content Outline
- The Wardens - seal the Veil permanently, at any cost
- The Architects - controlled manipulation, Ashfall was theirs
- The Threshold Initiative - adaptation is inevitable, humanity must change
- Inter-faction conflict - how it affects field operations
- Hunter mortality as a consequence of faction politics
- Closing Archivist annotation: *"Every Hunter who has ever died in the field died serving one of these agendas. Most of them never knew which one."*

## First Phantom Seed
Redacted signatory name appears on all three faction documents. Same length. Same position. One author approved all three faction charters.

## Intended Viewer Reaction
*"The Directorate isn't trying to protect people. It's trying to win an internal war."*
