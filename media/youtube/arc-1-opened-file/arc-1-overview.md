# Arc 1 Overview

## Arc Title
The Opened File

## Arc Premise
Someone inside the Hunters Directorate has begun leaking classified materials.
The leaks begin small - a single incident report, an entity file.
But as the videos progress, a pattern emerges beneath the surface.
Every incident, every entity, every recovered log points toward something the Directorate
has buried deeper than anything else in its archives.

By the end of Arc 1, the audience knows it exists.
They do not know what it is.

## Arc Emotional Journey
- Videos 01-03: Establish the world. Ground the viewer. Build trust in the format.
- Videos 04-06: Deepen the mythology. Introduce institutional conflict. Raise the stakes.
- Videos 07-09: Fracture the safety of the world. Show the Directorate failing.
- Video 10: Confirm The First Phantom is real. Leave everything else unanswered.

## Arc Payoff
A single leaked document - partially redacted - confirms the existence of an entity
the Directorate has never officially classified.
One line is left unredacted.
It reads: *"It was here before the first breach."*
