# SHORT 01 — Classified Transmission
## "Breach Confirmed"

### Format
Classified Transmission

### Connects To
The Veil / Veil Breaches / The Hunters Directorate (Video 01)

### Runtime Target
15–20 seconds

### Why This First
Lowest production barrier in the system — no Midjourney or Runway ML
needed. Tests your ElevenLabs and CapCut workflow before adding image
generation complexity. Establishes the Directorate's institutional voice
on short-form platforms immediately.

---

### Production Notes

**ElevenLabs Voice Treatment**
Two voices needed for this clip:
- Voice A (Dispatcher): flat, institutional, slightly degraded —
  sounds like a Directorate internal comms system
- Voice B (Field Operative): slightly more human, strained,
  processing what they're hearing

Heavy radio processing on both — narrow frequency range, static
bursts between lines, slight pitch instability on Voice B.

**CapCut Assembly**
- Pure black background throughout
- Classification stamp top center: OBSIDIAN FREQUENCY / TRANSMISSION
  INTERCEPTED / [DATE REDACTED]
- Text appears word by word in pale off-white (#DCE6D6) as audio plays —
  not instant, timed to voice
- Static burst visual effect between the two voices
- Ambient radio static runs under entire clip at low volume
- Hard cut to black and silence before transmission completes
- No music

---

**[BLACK SCREEN]**
**[Classification stamp fades in — top center]**
**[Text appears word by word as audio plays]**

# SHORT 02 — Animated Entity Reveal
## "HH-014"

### Format
Animated Entity Reveal

### Connects To
The Veil / Directorate (Video 01) — The Hollow Boy is Common tier,
appears in Region 1, connects to the world Video 01 establishes without
requiring a specific incident to be released yet.

### Runtime Target
12–18 seconds

### Why This Second
First use of the full Midjourney → Runway ML → CapCut pipeline.
The Hollow Boy is your lowest-threat entity — a child silhouette in a
doorway. Simple composition, strong atmosphere, easy to generate
consistently in Midjourney. Tests the pipeline on the least complex
entity before moving to higher-stakes reveals.

---

### Production Notes

**Midjourney Image Generation**
Use this prompt exactly:

```
small dark child-shaped silhouette standing in doorway of abandoned
farmhouse, viewed from end of long hallway, complete darkness behind
figure, single bare bulb light source above casting harsh downward light,
dust particles visible in air, warped wooden floorboards, peeling
wallpaper, rural decay, figure has no discernible features just shadow
in human shape, unsettling stillness, painterly dark illustration,
concept art style, highly detailed brushwork, textured paint surface
visible, cinematic dark fantasy illustration, in the style of a dark
graphic novel cover, teal and dark palette, deep shadow, high contrast
--ar 9:16 --style raw --stylize 800
```

Generate 4 variations. Select the one where:
- The figure is least defined (most shadow, least feature)
- The hallway depth is most pronounced
- The light source feels most practical and isolated

**Runway ML Animation**
- Motion: very slow push forward down the hallway toward figure
- Speed: barely perceptible — the viewer should feel they are
  approaching it without consciously noticing the camera move
- Duration: 8–10 seconds
- Add: subtle dust particle movement in light beam
- Do NOT add: figure movement — it must remain completely still

**ElevenLabs Audio**
No narration. Audio only:
- Low ambient hum — house settling, distant wind
- Barely audible electrical flicker from the bare bulb
- Complete silence in the final 2 seconds before cut

**CapCut Assembly**
- Runway ML clip plays
- Entity classification text fades in at 4 seconds:
  HH-014 / CLASSIFICATION: YELLOW / STATUS: ACTIVE
- Text holds for 3 seconds
- Hard cut to black — mid-motion, before the camera reaches the figure
- Silence

---

### On-Screen Text (CapCut)
```
HH-014
CLASSIFICATION: YELLOW
STATUS: ACTIVE
```

Pale off-white (#DCE6D6). Raleway Condensed font.
Fade in at 4 seconds. Hold. Hard cut takes it with the image.

### Caption (Platform Text)
```
entity file HH-014. recovered region 1. do not approach.

#ObsidianFrequency #HorrorShorts #AnalogHorror #fyp
```

### Pinned Comment
```
ENTITY HH-014 — THE HOLLOW BOY
THREAT LEVEL: YELLOW
REGION 1 SIGHTINGS: 14 CONFIRMED
DIRECTORATE STATUS: MONITORING
```

### First Phantom Seed
None — establishing short. Once character is established and audience
is familiar with entity file format, future entity reveals can carry
seeds embedded in the classification text (a case number, a cross-reference
to a file that doesn't exist yet, a redacted field).

# Production Order

Produce Short 01 (Classified Transmission) first — no image generation
needed, pure ElevenLabs and CapCut. Use this to master your text
animation and audio processing workflow in CapCut before adding
Midjourney and Runway ML to the pipeline.

Produce Short 02 (Animated Entity Reveal) second — first full pipeline
test. Take your time with the Midjourney prompt variations and the
Runway ML motion settings. The slow push toward the figure is the
entire effect — if it feels too fast, slow it down further.

Both clips are designed to publish alongside Video 01's launch per the
sequencing plan in the Short Form Content System document.
