# Headquarters System

## Status
Core Progression System

---

# Overview

Players maintain and upgrade a persistent Headquarters that serves as the central hub between expeditions.

The Headquarters is a living space that evolves over time based on player progression.

It represents:
- long-term progression
- resource investment
- narrative discovery
- and monster containment infrastructure

---

# Core Design Philosophy

The Headquarters is designed to provide:
- visible permanent progress
- gameplay utility upgrades
- collection display systems
- and narrative expansion

It reinforces the feeling that the player is building something meaningful within a haunted world.

---

# Headquarters Rooms

## Research Lab
Unlocks and expands monster data analysis.

- reveals entity weaknesses
- improves classification accuracy
- unlocks hidden lore entries

---

## Armory
Improves field equipment and hunter loadouts.

- unlocks advanced gear
- enhances expedition survivability
- enables specialized tools

---

## Containment Wing
Stores captured or documented creatures.

- displays contained entities
- unlocks behavioral insights
- enables experimental interactions

---

## Archive
Central lore repository.

- unlocks story fragments
- reveals incident reports
- expands world timeline

---

## Ritual Chamber
Unstable anomaly generation space.

- creates rare entity encounters
- influences expedition modifiers
- unlocks high-risk content

---

## Trophy Hall
Visual progression display.

- shows rare captures
- displays completed collections
- reinforces long-term achievement

---

# Progression Function

Each room upgrade:
- improves gameplay systems
- unlocks new content layers
- increases expedition efficiency
- and deepens lore access

---

# Design Intent

The Headquarters is a psychological anchor for players:
a place of control inside an increasingly uncontrollable world.
