# Seasonal Events System

## Status
Live Content System

---

# Overview

Seasonal events introduce time-limited content into the Obsidian Frequency universe.

These events provide:
- exclusive entities
- unique missions
- rare rewards
- and narrative variation

---

# Design Philosophy

Events reinforce the idea that:
> the Veil behaves differently under environmental and temporal conditions.

---

# October — Harvest Moon Event

## Theme
Agricultural horror and rural Veil corruption.

## Exclusive Entities
- Pumpkin Revenant
- Cornfield Scarecrow

## Gameplay Effects
- increased rural entity activity
- harvest-related anomalies
- ritual site emergence

---

# December — Winter Haunt

## Theme
Cold exposure and isolation horror.

## Effects
- reduced visibility
- frozen anomaly zones
- increased spirit manifestations

## Rewards
- exclusive containment artifacts
- winter-themed entities

---

# Spring — Cryptid Migration

## Theme
Rare entity movement across regions.

## Effects
- increased rare encounter rates
- cross-region entity appearances
- temporary migration routes

---

# Design Intent

Seasonal events:
- keep the world alive
- encourage return play
- introduce limited-time lore
- and expand entity diversity
