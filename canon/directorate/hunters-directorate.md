# The Hunters Directorate

## Classification
Paranormal Containment Organization

---

# Overview

The Hunters Directorate is a covert international organization tasked with:
- identifying Veil Breaches,
- containing entities,
- suppressing public awareness,
- and recovering anomalous materials.

The organization officially formed following the Black Vein Collapse in 1968.

---

# Primary Responsibilities

- Breach containment
- Entity investigation
- Hunter deployment
- Artifact recovery
- Research operations
- Civilian information suppression

---

# Internal Divisions

## Field Operations
Hunter deployment and breach response.

## Containment Division
Entity storage and suppression systems.

## Research Division
Veil experimentation and analysis.

## Recovery Division
Artifact retrieval and site excavation.

## Internal Oversight
Security and information control.

---

# Internal Conflict

The Directorate is heavily divided internally.

Major ideological factions include:

## The Wardens
Believe the Veil must remain sealed permanently.

## The Architects
Seek controlled Veil manipulation.

## The Threshold Initiative
Believe humanity must adapt to inevitable Veil integration.

---

# Public Status

Officially, the Directorate does not exist.

Most operations are concealed through:
- government partnerships,
- classified military programs,
- and industrial coverups.
