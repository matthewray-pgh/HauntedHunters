# Hunters of the Directorate

## Classification
Field Operative Program

---

# Overview

Hunters are specialized operatives deployed by the Hunters Directorate to investigate and contain Veil-related anomalies.

Each Hunter is trained in a specific discipline, allowing coordinated response to unpredictable paranormal environments.

---

# Functional Purpose

Hunters serve multiple operational roles:
- anomaly investigation
- entity containment support
- artifact retrieval
- breach stabilization
- and field documentation

---

# Operational Reality

Despite classification as elite operatives, Hunters are:
- highly vulnerable in the field
- often deployed with incomplete intelligence
- subject to high casualty rates
- and frequently lost or unaccounted for

---

# Psychological Impact

Long-term exposure to Veil Breaches may result in:
- memory degradation
- hallucinations
- personality fragmentation
- and entity resonance effects

---

# Directorate Classification

Hunters are considered:
EXPENDABLE BUT VALUABLE

Their survival probability is secondary to mission success and data recovery.
