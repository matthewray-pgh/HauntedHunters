# The Black Vein Collapse

## Date
1968

## Location
Appalachian Mining Region

## Classification
Major Historical Veil Incident

---

# Incident Summary

Mining crews excavating beneath the Black Vein site uncovered a subterranean structure inconsistent with known geological formations.

Survivors described:
- impossible tunnel geometry,
- echoing voices,
- and non-euclidean passageways.

Entire excavation teams disappeared shortly after initial breach exposure.

Federal recovery personnel later entered the site.

Most did not return.

---

# Reported Phenomena

- Tunnel expansion beyond mapped dimensions
- Auditory hallucinations
- Missing time events
- Psychological breakdowns
- Apparent spatial looping

---

# Historical Importance

The Black Vein Collapse directly contributed to the formation of the modern Hunters Directorate.

Many early containment doctrines originated from Black Vein survivor testimony.

---

# Current Site Status

Officially sealed.

Unofficial Directorate reports suggest:
the site remains active beneath containment barriers.

---

# Recovery Status

Several excavation records remain missing.
