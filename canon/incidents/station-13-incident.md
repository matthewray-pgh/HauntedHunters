# Station 13 Incident

## Date
1994

## Location
Blackwater Transit Authority

## Classification
Crimson-Level Veil Event

---

# Incident Summary

Transit expansion crews beneath Blackwater City uncovered an undocumented underground station absent from all official infrastructure records.

Shortly after excavation began:
- workers disappeared,
- station systems activated independently,
- and commuters across the city reported auditory hallucinations.

The underground section was quarantined following multiple disappearances.

---

# Hunter Deployment

Hunter Team Echo-4 entered the tunnels during initial containment operations.

Only fragmented recordings were recovered.
