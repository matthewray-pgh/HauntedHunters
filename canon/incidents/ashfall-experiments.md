# The Ashfall Experiments

## Date
1983

## Classification
Directorate Black Project

---

# Overview

The Ashfall Experiments were a series of unauthorized Directorate attempts to establish controlled interaction with the Veil.

The experiments were conducted within a remote subterranean research complex.

Official records regarding the project remain heavily redacted.

---

# Incident Outcome

Following activation of an experimental breach interface:
- communications failed,
- containment systems collapsed,
- and portions of the facility ceased behaving consistently with physical space.

Recovery teams reported:
- repeating corridors,
- faceless personnel,
- and distorted radio transmissions.

---

# Casualties

Unknown.

Personnel records related to Ashfall were erased from Directorate archives.

---

# Current Status

The facility is considered:
LOST

Some Directorate factions continue searching for surviving research data.

---

# Research Notes

Several modern Directorate technologies may originate from recovered Ashfall materials.

This remains classified.
