# HH-019 - The Lantern Warden

## Classification
Threshold Guardian Specter

## Rarity
Epic

## Threat Level
Red

## Status
Active - Location-Bound

## Manifestation Conditions
- Crossroads
- Bridge thresholds
- Property boundary markers of old homesteads

## Known Behaviors
- Appears as a tall figure in a long coat carrying a rusted lantern
- Does not speak - communicates through lantern behavior (flicker patterns)
- Will not cross thresholds it guards
- Entities in proximity appear suppressed or weakened while Warden is present

## Environmental Effects
- Other paranormal activity decreases in radius
- All artificial light sources shift to amber tone
- Compass always points toward Warden regardless of orientation

## Related Locations
- Rural Pennsylvania crossroads - Region 1
- Old homestead boundary sites

## Related Incidents
- Referenced in Black Vein survivor testimony - described as "the one that watches the door"

## Hunter Notes
> "It wasn't threatening us. It was warning us. There's a difference. We learned that too late."

## Gameplay Mechanics
- Suppresses other entity activity in zone - can be used strategically
- Cannot be captured through standard methods
- Medium class required to interpret lantern communication

- Lore breakdown - threshold guardian mythology vs. Directorate classification
- Field footage: lantern light pattern analysis
