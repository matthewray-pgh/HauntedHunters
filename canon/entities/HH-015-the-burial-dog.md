# HH-015 - The Burial Dog

## Classification
Territorial Specter

## Rarity
Common

## Threat Level
Yellow

## Status
Active

## Manifestation Conditions
- Old cemeteries and burial grounds
- Locations of unmarked graves
- Disturbed soil sites

## Known Behaviors
- Heard before seen - low, rhythmic growling beneath the ground
- Manifests as a large dog-shaped mass of dark mist
- Patrols perimeter of burial sites aggressively
- Does not cross into structures - only open ground

## Environmental Effects
- Soil disturbance without geological cause
- Animal remains found near perimeter
- Unusual digging patterns around grave markers

## Related Locations
- Rural Pennsylvania - Region 1 cemeteries
- Abandoned homestead burial plots

## Related Incidents
- None classified. Consistent field reports from Region 1 survey teams.

## Hunter Notes
> "Stay on the path. Stay on the path and don't look into the tree line."

## Gameplay Mechanics
- Passive until perimeter is crossed
- Aggressive pursuit in open areas
- Loses tracking ability inside enclosed structures

## Video Concepts
- Night vision cemetery walkthrough
- Audio log of low-frequency growl captured on EMF equipment
