# HH-014 - The Hollow Boy

## Classification
Residual Apparition

## Rarity
Common

## Threat Level
Yellow

## Status
Active - Multiple Confirmed Sightings

## Manifestation Conditions
- Abandoned farmhouses
- Empty children's bedrooms
- Locations with a history of childhood illness or neglect

## Known Behaviors
- Appears as a small, featureless silhouette in doorways or at the end of hallways
- Does not move when observed directly
- Disappears when light source is brought closer
- Occasionally mimics childlike sounds - humming, crying, dragging toys

## Environmental Effects
- Sudden cold pockets at ground level
- Toys or small objects found displaced
- Rocking chairs or furniture moving without cause

## Related Locations
- Rural Pennsylvania farmsteads
- Abandoned rural schoolhouses

## Related Incidents
- None confirmed. Multiple unofficial field reports reference similar sightings across Region 1.

## Hunter Notes
> "It just stands there. It doesn't want anything from you. That's the part that gets under your skin."

## Gameplay Mechanics
- Does not engage unless cornered
- Flees toward dark areas when approached
- Capture difficulty increases significantly in darkness

## Video Concepts
- First-person farmhouse walkthrough - entity glimpsed at end of hallway
- Field report read-aloud with blurred photo evidence
