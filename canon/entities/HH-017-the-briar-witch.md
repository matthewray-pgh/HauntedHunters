# HH-017 - The Briar Witch

## Classification
Territorial Nature Aberration

## Rarity
Rare

## Threat Level
Orange

## Status
Active - Seasonal Fluctuation

## Manifestation Conditions
- Dense forest locations
- Areas of overgrown infrastructure
- Sites of old homesteads reclaimed by vegetation

## Known Behaviors
- Never fully visible - perceived only through movement of vegetation
- Leaves effigies constructed from branches, animal bones, and wire
- Disables compass and GPS navigation within proximity radius
- Herds targets toward dense undergrowth

## Environmental Effects
- Unnatural thorn growth along pathways
- Animal silence across wide radius
- Trail markers found reversed or destroyed

## Related Locations
- Region 1 - rural forest zones
- Abandoned homestead territories

## Related Incidents
- None classified.

## Hunter Notes
> "The trees move when it moves. You learn to watch the tree line, not the ground."

## Gameplay Mechanics
- Navigation tools disabled in proximity zone
- Cannot be directly observed - tracked through environmental cues only
- Cryptozoologist class required for stable capture attempt

## Video Concepts
- Trail cam footage of effigy construction in time-lapse
- Field team audio log - navigation failure in forest zone
