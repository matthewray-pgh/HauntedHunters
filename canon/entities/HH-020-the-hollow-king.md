# HH-020 - The Hollow King

## Classification
Veil-Emergent Apex Entity

## Rarity
Legendary

## Threat Level
Obsidian

## Status
Uncontained

## Manifestation Conditions
- Level IV or higher Veil Breaches
- Locations with catastrophic historical trauma
- Sites where multiple entities have been destroyed in proximity

## Known Behaviors
- Appears as an impossibly tall, antlered humanoid figure composed of dead wood, bone, and dark mist
- Does not pursue - waits
- All entities within significant radius fall into passive or dormant states in its presence
- Speaking in its presence causes immediate auditory hallucinations in all team members
- Has been observed standing motionless for recorded periods exceeding 72 hours

## Environmental Effects
- Veil Breach severity escalates passively in proximity
- Plant life dies in expanding radius
- Time perception anomalies reported by all witnesses
- No electronic equipment functions reliably

## Related Locations
- Deep Appalachian wilderness - Region 1 extreme boundary
- Suspected presence at Black Vein lower excavation site

## Related Incidents
- Black Vein Collapse (1968) - unverified reference in redacted survivor testimony
- Mentioned in Ashfall recovered fragments as "the one the breach opens for"

## Hunter Notes
> "Pull back. Pull everyone back. It doesn't need to move. It just needs to exist near you long enough."

## Gameplay Mechanics
- Cannot be captured - documentation only
- Triggers passive Veil escalation affecting all active missions
- Encountering it unlocks permanent lore entries and hidden Directorate archive fragments

- Lore video: "What Is The Hollow King?" - mythology, sightings, Directorate silence
- Dramatic reading of redacted Black Vein survivor testimony
- Entity tier breakdown placing it above all known classifications
