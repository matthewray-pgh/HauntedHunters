# HH-016 - The Miner's Echo

## Classification
Trauma-Bound Remnant

## Rarity
Rare

## Threat Level
Orange

## Status
Active - Confirmed Subterranean

## Manifestation Conditions
- Collapsed mine shafts
- Underground tunnels
- Locations connected to mining disasters

## Known Behaviors
- Appears as a translucent figure in full mining gear, repeating identical movements on a loop
- Loop always ends in a violent death re-enactment before resetting
- Responds to sound - pauses loop and orients toward noise source
- Will approach if disturbed three times consecutively

## Environmental Effects
- Pickaxe strikes audible through rock walls with no physical source
- Oxygen levels fluctuate in confined spaces
- Lanterns and flashlights dim progressively

## Related Locations
- Black Vein Collapse site
- Region 1 - Appalachian mine network

## Related Incidents
- Black Vein Collapse (1968)

## Hunter Notes
> "Don't make noise. Whatever you do, don't make noise. It's not angry. It's just... replaying."

## Gameplay Mechanics
- Non-aggressive unless disturbed
- Sound-sensitive - equipment use triggers attention
- Capture window opens during loop reset phase

## Video Concepts
- Classified incident reconstruction - silent footage of figure repeating loop
- Audio comparison: recorded pickaxe sounds vs. baseline geological noise
