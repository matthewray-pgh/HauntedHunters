# Veil Breaches

## Overview

Veil Breaches are locations where the barrier between stable reality and the Veil has weakened.

Breaches vary significantly in severity.

Minor breaches may produce:
- unexplained sounds,
- electrical disturbances,
- and hallucinations.

Major breaches may cause:
- impossible geometry,
- severe entity manifestation,
- memory instability,
- and full environmental corruption.

---

# Common Breach Locations

The Directorate has identified increased breach activity in areas associated with:
- mass trauma,
- industrial disasters,
- underground structures,
- abandoned institutions,
- and prolonged human suffering.

Examples include:
- mines,
- transit tunnels,
- hospitals,
- laboratories,
- churches,
- and disaster sites.

---

# Breach Severity Levels

## Level I
Minor disturbances.

## Level II
Localized manifestations.

## Level III
Persistent entity activity.

## Level IV
Reality instability.

## Level V
Catastrophic Veil integration.

---

# Important Observation

Breaches appear influenced by human psychological conditions.

Environments with intense emotional history frequently generate highly specialized entity manifestations.

---

# Directorate Protocol

Standard civilian cover stories include:
- gas leaks,
- structural instability,
- contamination,
- terrorist threats,
- or industrial accidents.
