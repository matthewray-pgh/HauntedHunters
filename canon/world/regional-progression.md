# Regional Progression

## Status
World Structure System

---

# Overview

The world of Haunted Hunters is divided into escalating regions.

Each region represents:
- increased Veil instability
- higher entity complexity
- and deeper lore exposure

---

# Region 1 — Rural Pennsylvania

## Overview
The starting region. Quiet, isolated, and heavily decayed.

## Environment Types
- farms
- forests
- abandoned mines
- rural towns

## Characteristics
- low entity density
- grounded horror
- early anomaly exposure

---

# Region 2 — Industrial Ruins

## Overview
Collapsed industrial infrastructure and urban decay.

## Environment Types
- factories
- warehouses
- sewer systems
- power plants

## Characteristics
- increased entity complexity
- mechanical anomalies
- environmental corruption

---

# Region 3 — Forgotten Coast

## Overview
Coastal decay zones influenced by maritime abandonment.

## Environment Types
- shipwrecks
- marshes
- lighthouses
- abandoned docks

## Characteristics
- water-based anomalies
- distorted navigation
- isolation effects

---

# Region 4 — The Veil

## Overview
A non-physical reality layer.

## Characteristics
- impossible geometry
- direct entity manifestations
- unstable physics
- reality distortion events

## Status
Endgame content layer

---

# Design Intent

Progression is not just difficulty scaling.

It is:
> deeper exposure to reality instability.
