# The Veil

## Classification
Dimensional Phenomenon

## Overview

The Veil is the hidden layer beneath stable human reality.

It is not considered a separate universe, afterlife, or alternate dimension in the traditional sense. Directorate researchers describe it as:

> "Reality underneath reality."

The Veil appears intrinsically linked to:
- human consciousness,
- memory,
- trauma,
- emotion,
- death,
- and perception.

Under normal conditions, humanity remains isolated from direct interaction with the Veil.

However, certain environments and events weaken the boundary between layers of reality.

These weakened regions are classified as:
## Veil Breaches

---

# Known Effects of Veil Exposure

- Distorted architecture
- Hallucinations
- Audio anomalies
- Temporal inconsistencies
- Memory corruption
- Entity manifestations
- Psychological degradation

---

# Research Notes

The Directorate does not fully understand:
- whether the Veil is sentient,
- whether entities originate naturally within it,
- or whether humanity itself influences its behavior.

Several internal Directorate factions believe the Veil predates human civilization entirely.

---

# Important Theory

Some researchers believe consciousness itself may act as a stabilizing force against the Veil.

If true, widespread breach events could fundamentally destabilize reality.
